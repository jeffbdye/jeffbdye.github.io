About

Hey! I'm a software engineer focused on delivering the right solution for the customer. It starts with empathy to better understand what needs to be built (and what doesn't). Execution best happens iteratively, where teams can ship high quality changes consistently and confidently. Successful delivery relies on careful observability of systems and how users accomplish their goals. We should always be looking to learn and improve, while constructively questioning the norms in order to solve each new problem accordingly.

Professional background

I've been fortunate enough to work on a variety of projects in my time at Blackbaud building SaaS products to help nonprofit organizations operate more effectively in the many ways they bring about good to the world. I've gotten to learn about fundraising analytics, email engagement/interactions, event registration/management, international contact/address management, and most recently on our DevOps + Azure cloud architecture team working to empower all of Blackbaud's product teams in delivering on that same mission. I've been able to collaborate with teams around the world as both an individual contributor and development lead.
